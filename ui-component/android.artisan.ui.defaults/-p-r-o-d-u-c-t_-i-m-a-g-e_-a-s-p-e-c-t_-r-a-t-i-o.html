PRODUCT_IMAGE_ASPECT_RATIO

const val PRODUCT_IMAGE_ASPECT_RATIO: Float = 0.75f

The aspect ratio (width/height) of product images.

This constant defines the desired aspect ratio for product images displayed within the application. It's used to maintain consistency in the visual presentation of product images, ensuring they are displayed with a specific proportion.

For instance, an aspect ratio of 0.75 implies that the width of the image should be 75% of its height, i.e., for every 100 pixels of height, the width will be 75 pixels. This can be considered as a portrait orientation.

Value: 0.75f
